### Slope
$["slope" = m = (y_2-y_2)/(x_2-x_1)]

### Standard Form
$[ax + by = c]

### Slope Intercept
$[y = mx+b]

### Point-Slope
$[y - y_1 = m (x- x_1)]

### Horizontal Line
$[y = c]

- $["slope" = 0]

### Vertical Line
$[x = c]

- $["slope" = "undefined"]

### x-intercept
$[(x, 0)]

### y-intercept
$[(0, y)]

### Parallel Lines 
$[m_1 = m_2]

### Perindicular lines
$[m_1 = 1/m_2] or $[m_1 * m_2 = -1]
